How to read this. Bars show the full response distribution. On Q2 the Optimism Mean is reverse-adjusted so higher always = more AI-optimistic (negatively-worded items flipped, 6 − mean); Q1 shows the raw automation mean (1–5). “Not sure” is treated as the neutral midpoint. The coloured left strip marks statement wording (green = positively worded, red = negatively worded). The Overall AI-optimism composite is a weighted average of the Q2 items (weights in AGREE_WEIGHTS). Significant factors come from a multivariate linear regression (Age, SD expertise, AI familiarity z-scored; Male and North America binary): solid chip = p<0.05, lighter chip = p<0.10. Green ▲ = scores higher, Red ▼ = scores lower. With a small sample, treat these as exploratory.
